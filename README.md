## Projects

### 1. US Logistics Shipment Cost Analysis
**Tools:** Google Sheets | **Date:** May 2026

Analyzed 2,002 shipment records across a 10-warehouse network to identify
cost inefficiencies by warehouse and carrier. Engineered a Cost_per_kg
metric and used pivot tables to reveal that Warehouse_HOU was the most
expensive at $20.48/kg — nearly double the most efficient warehouse.
Recommended shifting volume to USPS and Amazon Logistics.

### 2. Mobile Device Sales Analysis — Regional Distribution Performance
**Tools:** Google Sheets | **Date:** May 2026

Cleaned and analyzed 50,000 rows of mobile device sales data across
5 regions in India. Built pivot tables to uncover revenue and product
mix issues in Central and East regions. Developed 3 actionable
recommendations to improve regional performance.
